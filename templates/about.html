What is ODETTA?

The Online Database of Extragalactic Transient Theoretical Astrophysics (ODETTA) was created to share synthetic spectra resulting from radiative transfer simulations with the larger astrophysics community. As observers have started capturing transient events at a record pace, the interpretation of spectra has suddenly become critical to the both the short-term goals of follow-up programs trying to push the limits of how fast and frequently the scientific community can observe transients, as well as the long-term goals of science groups hoping to better understand the explosions.
